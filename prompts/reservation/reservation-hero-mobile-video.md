# Reservation Hero Mobile Video

## Purpose
Describe what this prompt generates.

## Version
Draft

## Prompt
```text
TITLE: Giardini Café — Cinematic Reservation Hero

FORMAT:
- Duration: 8–10 seconds
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (safe center for mobile)
- Loop: seamless
- Style: cinematic, ultra-realistic, high-end

REFERENCE:
Use the selected image of an open book on a wooden table with a dark green background and a warm diagonal light beam.

CONCEPT:
A refined, cinematic transition that connects the act of reservation with the brand experience — subtle, elegant, intentional.

SCENE STRUCTURE:

1. Opening (0–2s)
- static composition based on the reference image
- deep green background, warm golden light beam
- camera starts slightly above the book (top-down angle, around 20–30 degrees)
- very subtle push-in from above, gently approaching the book
- floating dust particles visible in the light

2. Activation (2–4s)
- light intensity slightly increases on the book
- page reacts subtly (minimal organic motion)
- atmosphere becomes more immersive
- camera continues slow top-down push-in (never frontal)

3. Action (4–6s)
- a refined ink-like stroke begins forming across the page
- movement is smooth, controlled and elegant (not cartoonish)
- stroke behaves like liquid pigment on paper (2D surface interaction)
- color: dark gold / deep green
- IMPORTANT: ink must stay flat on the page (no volumetric smoke, no 3D fluid, no vapor)
- as the ink stabilizes, a subtle minimal mark appears briefly on the page (a refined circular seal or small check-like symbol)
- the mark should feel premium and almost hidden, not explicit or illustrative
- it fades naturally as the transition begins

4. Transition (6–8s)
- the ink spreads across the surface of the page
- transforms into a smooth, continuous layer
- expands outward cleanly, like pigment saturating paper
- gradually fills the frame with deep green
- IMPORTANT: no smoke, no mist, no floating fluid — only surface spreading

5. Reveal (8–10s)
- background becomes clean, premium dark green
- subtle particles remain
- preserve a strong cinematic final moment (visual climax)
- no visible text or typography
- final frame should feel like a stage ready for overlay content

STYLE & DIRECTION:
- slow, controlled motion
- cinematic lighting
- high contrast (light vs dark)
- minimal and refined

COLOR PALETTE:
- deep green (#0f2b23 / #0b1f1a)
- warm gold highlights
- natural wood tones (muted)

CAMERA:
- slow push-in from a slightly top-down perspective
- no cuts, no abrupt movement

AVOID:
- smoke effects
- vapor or mist
- volumetric fluid simulation
- exaggerated magic effects
- glitch transitions
- fast motion
- cartoon-like writing
- overuse of particles
- overly bright highlights

MOOD:
- sophisticated
- calm
- immersive
- intentional

GOAL:
Create a premium hero video that visually represents the act of reserving an experience — subtle, elegant and aligned with a luxury café brand, with a strong final frame designed for UI overlay.
```
