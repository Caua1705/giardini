## Purpose

## Prompt
```text
A single continuous cinematic shot inside a premium cozy café.

Scene starts with a static camera focused on a round wooden table near a large window with soft sheer curtains. Natural warm sunlight fills the space.

On the table:
- a ceramic cup of coffee with subtle steam
- a closed book
- a small notebook

A young woman (around 22 years old, brunette, natural appearance, realistic body, no exaggerated beauty) is already seated at the table.

She slowly places her hand on the notebook and gently opens it. Her movements are calm and natural.

After 2–3 seconds, a second woman (around 25 years old, blonde, natural and realistic appearance) arrives naturally from the side of the frame, walking into the scene (no sudden appearance). She sits down across the table.

They briefly exchange a subtle glance and a small smile — very natural, no exaggerated acting.

After that, a waitress (natural appearance, professional posture) walks into the scene from the background, approaching the table smoothly (no teleporting, no sudden cuts).

The waitress gently places two cups of coffee on the table and says something softly (no audio needed, just natural lip movement).

One of the women crosses her legs casually and picks up the book, starting to read.

Camera:
- completely static (no zoom, no camera movement)
- medium framing (table + upper body)
- shallow depth of field (background softly blurred)

Lighting:
- natural, warm, cinematic
- soft shadows
- golden tone, late afternoon feel

Style:
- ultra realistic
- cinematic
- no artificial or surreal elements
- no exaggerated expressions
- no sudden movements

Motion rules:
- all characters must enter the scene naturally (no popping in)
- movements must be slow and realistic
- no jump cuts, no transitions

Mood:
calm, elegant, intimate, real-life café moment

Duration: 8–10 seconds
Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:5
High realism, premium brand aesthetic
```
